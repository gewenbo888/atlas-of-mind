# The Atlas of Mind · 思维之图

A bilingual single-page digital institution dedicated to the brain, thinking methods, mental models, learning science, decision-making, creativity, consciousness, and the future of intelligence.

## Sections (12)

01. Manifesto — why this exists
02. Numbers — the mind in scale (animated stat counters: 86B neurons, 100T synapses, 20W power, etc.)
03. The Brain — most complex object we know of
04. Thinking Methods — six tools (Analytical · Critical · Systems · First-Principles · Lateral · Probabilistic)
05. Mental Models — eight reusable lenses (Inversion · Second-Order Effects · Opportunity Cost · Margin of Safety · Power Laws · Compounding · Bayesian Updating · Local vs Global Optima)
06. Cognition & Consciousness — six rungs from neurons to qualia, three contemporary theories
07. Decision — Kahneman System 1 vs System 2
08. Creativity — divergent + convergent
09. Brain & AI — the two intelligences in conversation
10. **Interactive Map of Mind** — clickable SVG with 9 nodes (Self center + Perception · Memory · Attention · Emotion · Reasoning · Language · Imagination · Action)
11. Timeline — 11 milestones from Socrates to 2026
12. FAQ — 5 honest answers
+ Newsletter (with toast notification)
+ Multi-column footer with cross-links to sister psyverse sites

## Features

- **Toggle bilingual** EN ↔ 中文 with localStorage persistence
- **Dark/light theme** toggle with localStorage persistence
- **Animated neural network background** — particle field with connecting edges
- **Animated stat counters** that fire on scroll
- **Interactive Map of Mind SVG** — click nodes to see definitions in current language
- **Scroll-reveal** section animations
- **Toast notification** on newsletter submit
- **Back-to-top** button
- Mobile-responsive

## Aesthetic

Cormorant Garamond italic display + Source Serif body + Noto Serif/Sans SC + JetBrains Mono labels. Deep `#08080d` (dark) / `#fafaf2` (light parchment). Quantum gold (`#d4af37`) primary + neural cyan (`#7dd3fc`) + warm amber + rose + emerald accents. Scholarly + futuristic + monumental.
